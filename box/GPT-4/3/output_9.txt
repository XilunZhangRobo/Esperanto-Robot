Plan:

1. Open the gripper.
2. Get the graspable point of cubeA.
3. Move to the position above cubeA.
4. Move down to grasp cubeA.
5. Close the gripper to grasp cubeA.
6. Lift cubeA.
7. Get the size of cubeB.
8. Move to the position above cubeB.
9. Move down to place cubeA on top of cubeB.
10. Open the gripper to release cubeA.
11. Lift the gripper.
12. Get the graspable point of ballA.
13. Move to the position above ballA.
14. Move down to grasp ballA.
15. Close the gripper to grasp ballA.
16. Lift ballA.
17. Get the size of cubeA.
18. Move to the position above cubeA (which is now on top of cubeB).
19. Move down to place ballA on top of cubeA.
20. Open the gripper to release ballA.
21. Lift the gripper to finish the task.

Python Code:

```python
import numpy as np

# Step 1
open_gripper()

# Step 2
cubeA_graspable_point = get_graspable_point('cubeA')

# Step 3
move_to_position(cubeA_graspable_point + np.array([0, 0, 1]))

# Step 4
move_to_position(cubeA_graspable_point)

# Step 5
close_gripper()

# Step 6
move_to_position(cubeA_graspable_point + np.array([0, 0, 1]))

# Step 7
cubeB_size = get_size('cubeB')

# Step 8
move_to_position(cubeB_size + np.array([0, 0, 1]))

# Step 9
move_to_position(cubeB_size)

# Step 10
open_gripper()

# Step 11
move_to_position(cubeB_size + np.array([0, 0, 1]))

# Step 12
ballA_graspable_point = get_graspable_point('ballA')

# Step 13
move_to_position(ballA_graspable_point + np.array([0, 0, 1]))

# Step 14
move_to_position(ballA_graspable_point)

# Step 15
close_gripper()

# Step 16
move_to_position(ballA_graspable_point + np.array([0, 0, 1]))

# Step 17
cubeA_size = get_size('cubeA')

# Step 18
move_to_position(cubeA_size + np.array([0, 0, 1]))

# Step 19
move_to_position(cubeA_size)

# Step 20
open_gripper()

# Step 21
move_to_position(cubeA_size + np.array([0, 0, 1]))
```
Please note that the above code assumes that the z-axis is the vertical direction and the gripper is initially at a position higher than all objects. The "+ np.array([0, 0, 1])" is used to move the gripper above the object before grasping or placing it.
