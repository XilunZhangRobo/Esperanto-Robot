Plan:

1. Open the gripper.
2. Get the graspable point of cubeA.
3. Move to the position above cubeA.
4. Move down to grasp cubeA.
5. Close the gripper to grasp cubeA.
6. Lift cubeA up.
7. Get the size of cubeB.
8. Move to the position above cubeB.
9. Move down to place cubeA on top of cubeB.
10. Open the gripper to release cubeA.
11. Lift the gripper up.
12. Get the graspable point of ballA.
13. Move to the position above ballA.
14. Move down to grasp ballA.
15. Close the gripper to grasp ballA.
16. Lift ballA up.
17. Move to the position above cubeA (which is now on top of cubeB).
18. Move down to place ballA on top of cubeA.
19. Open the gripper to release ballA.

Python Code:

```python
import numpy as np

# Step 1
open_gripper()

# Step 2
cubeA_graspable_point = get_graspable_point('cubeA')

# Step 3
move_to_position(np.array([cubeA_graspable_point[0], cubeA_graspable_point[1], cubeA_graspable_point[2] + 0.1]))

# Step 4
move_to_position(cubeA_graspable_point)

# Step 5
close_gripper()

# Step 6
move_to_position(np.array([cubeA_graspable_point[0], cubeA_graspable_point[1], cubeA_graspable_point[2] + 0.1]))

# Step 7
cubeB_size = get_size('cubeB')

# Step 8
move_to_position(np.array([cubeB_size[0], cubeB_size[1], cubeB_size[2] + 0.1]))

# Step 9
move_to_position(np.array([cubeB_size[0], cubeB_size[1], cubeB_size[2]]))

# Step 10
open_gripper()

# Step 11
move_to_position(np.array([cubeB_size[0], cubeB_size[1], cubeB_size[2] + 0.1]))

# Step 12
ballA_graspable_point = get_graspable_point('ballA')

# Step 13
move_to_position(np.array([ballA_graspable_point[0], ballA_graspable_point[1], ballA_graspable_point[2] + 0.1]))

# Step 14
move_to_position(ballA_graspable_point)

# Step 15
close_gripper()

# Step 16
move_to_position(np.array([ballA_graspable_point[0], ballA_graspable_point[1], ballA_graspable_point[2] + 0.1]))

# Step 17
move_to_position(np.array([cubeB_size[0], cubeB_size[1], cubeB_size[2] + cubeA_graspable_point[2] + 0.1]))

# Step 18
move_to_position(np.array([cubeB_size[0], cubeB_size[1], cubeB_size[2] + cubeA_graspable_point[2]]))

# Step 19
open_gripper()
```

Note: The "+ 0.1" in the move_to_position function is to ensure that the gripper is slightly above the object before moving down to grasp it. This is to prevent the gripper from pushing the object away.
